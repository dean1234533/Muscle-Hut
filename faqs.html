Just bring yourself, some comfortable workout clothes, and trainers. We have lockers and changing rooms on-site. You can bring your own padlock or rent one from the front desk. Towels are available to purchase at reception, or bring your own.

Members must be 16 or over to use the gym. Members aged 16–17 require written parental consent and must complete an induction with a parent or guardian present. Under-16s may participate in specific supervised classes only — contact us for details.

Yes. All our memberships are monthly rolling contracts. You can cancel at any time with 30 days written notice — no cancellation fees, no lengthy exit process. We believe in earning your membership every single month.

We're currently waiving our joining fee for all new members this month. Just your first month's membership is due when you sign up. This offer is limited — contact us to confirm it's still available before you visit.

Yes, we offer discounted rates for students (valid student ID required) and family memberships for couples or families joining together. Contact us directly and we'll put together a custom quote for you.

Yes. If you're going on holiday, dealing with an injury, or just need a break, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year. There's no charge for the first freeze. Just let us know in advance and we'll sort it out for you.

Absolutely. You can change your membership plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect from your next billing date. Just speak to a member of staff or email us.

We're open Monday to Friday 6am to Midnight, and Saturday and Sunday 8am to 8pm. We're open 365 days a year including bank holidays, so there's truly never an excuse not to train.

There is free street parking available on High Road Leytonstone and surrounding streets. We're also a short 8-minute walk from Leytonstone Underground Station (Central Line), making us easily accessible from all over East London.

Yes. We have completely separate changing rooms, showers and locker areas for men and women. Both are maintained to the highest standards of cleanliness and are deep-cleaned daily.

Yes — we have a fully stocked nutrition store and a protein bar on-site. You can pick up supplements, pre-workout, protein powders, healthy snacks and fresh shakes before, during, or after your session. Pro members get 10% off and Elite members get 20% off all store purchases.

Yes. We have several certified personal trainers available for 1-on-1 sessions. PT sessions are included in our Pro (2 per month) and Elite (unlimited) memberships. If you're on a Basic plan, you can book PT sessions as an add-on. Contact us for current PT rates.

Yes. We run a full timetable of group classes including HIIT, boxing circuits, spin, yoga, and more. Classes are included with Pro and Elite memberships. Basic members can join classes for a small drop-in fee. Check our schedule at reception or contact us for the latest timetable.
