COACH ADVICE

Tips to Maximise Every Plan

01

Follow the Plan — Exactly

Resist the urge to swap exercises or skip sessions. The plan is progressive — every session builds on the last. Trust the process for at least 4 weeks before judging it.

02

Track Everything

Write down every weight, every rep, every set. Progress is invisible without data. Our members who track consistently see 2–3x better results than those who don't.

03

Nutrition is 70%

You cannot out-train a bad diet. Match your nutrition to your goal — surplus for muscle gain, deficit for fat loss. Use the guide included with your plan as a starting point.

04

Sleep Is Training

Muscle is built at rest, not in the gym. Aim for 7–9 hours per night. If you're sleeping less than 6 hours, no programme will get you to your goal faster.

05

Ask for Help

Our coaches are on the floor every session. If you're unsure about a movement or your form, just ask — that's what we're here for. Nobody will judge you for learning.

06

Be Consistent Over Perfect

3 consistent sessions per week beats 5 "perfect" sessions that you burn out on. Build the habit first, then build the intensity. Consistency is the only strategy that works long-term.
