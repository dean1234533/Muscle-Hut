A Community.

We've been transforming bodies and lives in Leytonstone for over 10 years. Find out who we are and what drives us every single day.

OUR STORY

Built on Passion

Da Gym was founded over 10 years ago with one simple idea: create a gym where everyone feels welcome, supported, and challenged. Not a faceless chain. Not intimidating. Just a proper gym with real people who care.

From a single gym floor in Leytonstone, we've grown into one of East London's most trusted fitness communities — with over 300 active members and a team of expert coaches who live and breathe fitness.

From complete beginners finding their feet, to competitive athletes pushing their limits — there is a place here for you.
